About P.S. Coffee

Built for people who drink
coffee to get things done.

P.S. Coffee is a specialty coffee QSR operating inside Ahmedabad's co-working spaces, corporate offices, and university campuses. 100% Arabica. From ₹89. In under 90 seconds.

P.S.

The mission is simple.
Embarrassingly so.

Specialty coffee — 100% Arabica, properly sourced, properly made — at a price the person who actually needs it every day can afford. ₹89. Not ₹320.

We are not a lifestyle brand. We are not building a destination café. We are building a habit, at the scale of a city, inside the buildings where that city actually works.

The model is lean by design. Small-format decks. Focused menu. Direct sourcing. The result is specialty grade coffee at the price of an ordinary cup — and it's a model we plan to take further than Ahmedabad.

P.S. — Plain & Simple. That's the whole brief.

In one sentence

"We're building the coffee habit for India's working generation — the people who drink it not for a photograph, but for a Tuesday."

What we've committed to

The P.S. standard.
Publicly stated. Always kept.

We will never put Robusta in a P.S. cup.

We will never use a blend we can't name.

We will never charge a premium for a mediocre bean.

We will never add a surcharge for oat milk.

We will never raise the price without telling you why.

We will never auto-renew a Pack subscription without your explicit go-ahead.
