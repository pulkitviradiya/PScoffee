03 — Why "Pods"

Not cafes.
Not kiosks. Pods.

A pod is the smallest self-contained unit of something essential. A coffee pod. A sleep pod. A growth pod. It does exactly one thing, completely.

Each Pod is a complete specialty coffee operation in 50–200 sq ft. It fits where a photocopier used to be. It's there because we measured foot traffic, not because we liked the neighbourhood.

A gym-adjacent Pod captures the early morning. A co-working Pod captures mid-day. A campus Pod captures evenings. The goal is always the same — to be the nearest quality cup to wherever you are, at whatever hour you need one.
