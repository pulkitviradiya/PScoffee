The P.S. standard — on every cup.

All espresso drinks are made with 100% Arabica single-origin beans from Chikmagalur or Coorg. Oat milk is available at every deck — no surcharge, ever. Whole milk is the default. Soy on request at select locations.

Seasonal single-origin rotations are listed at the deck. When the bean changes, the name changes with it. We'll always tell you what's in the cup.

P.S. — We don't do decaf. We do Arabica, properly. If you want half the coffee, order an Americano.
