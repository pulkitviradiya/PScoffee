What the app does

Built around the habit.
Not around the app.

The P.S. app isn't a loyalty programme with seventeen tabs. It does three things well, and gets out of the way.

Order ahead.
Skip the wait.

Pre-order your cup before you arrive. It's ready at the deck when you walk in. No queue. No decision at the counter. No lost 4 minutes you didn't have.

P.S. — The record is 11 seconds from door to cup.

Manage your
P.S. Pack.

Top up your 10 or 20 cup pack, check your balance, and redeem by scanning at the deck. No physical card. No lost slip. Your pack is always with you.

P.S. — No expiry. No auto-renewal without your say.

Track your
streak.

Your daily cup counter, visualised. Watch the streak grow. See which days you made it. The habit you can feel is more honest than the one you're just told about.

P.S. — Day 30 has a thing. You'll see.

Getting started

Download. Set up.
That's genuinely it.

P.S. = Plain & Simple. The app takes about 90 seconds to set up — which is exactly as long as it takes to make your first cup.

1

Download the app

Available on iOS and Android. Free. No account required to browse. Sign up when you're ready to order.

2

Find your deck

The app shows all active P.S. decks near you — with live hours and your estimated walk time.

3

Order ahead or scan

Pre-order before you arrive or scan to pay at the deck. If you have a Pack, it deducts automatically.

4

Show up. Collect.

Walk in. Your cup is on the counter. Walk out. Repeat tomorrow. That's the whole system.
